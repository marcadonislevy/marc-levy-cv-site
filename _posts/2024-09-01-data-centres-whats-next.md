This short piece accompanies a LinkedIn post on the future of data centres.

- Growth: multiple gigawatts of capacity now in construction or planning across EMEA.
- Pressure points: grid connection, land, sustainability, skills and regulation.
- Opportunity: better commercial models, digital project controls and AI-supported decision-making.
