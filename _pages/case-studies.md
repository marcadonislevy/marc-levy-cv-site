This page highlights selected case studies across transport, data centres, rail, water and economic development.

## Strategic and Technical Transformation at Transport for London (TfL)

As Senior Strategic Technical Advisor in the London Underground Line Upgrade and Capital Projects directorate, I:

- Developed terms of reference and business planning documents  
- Ran time and motion studies and headcount analysis to boost productivity  
- Designed an organisational structure aligned with project delivery goals  
- Created asset unit-cost models and efficiency workstreams  
- Benchmarked project management performance and costs  

**Result:** A structured approach to efficiency, clearer KPIs, and a centre of excellence model for continuous improvement.

## Google Eemshaven Data Centre, Netherlands

Leading a new hyperscale data centre build for Google in Eemshaven, I:

- Led a multidisciplinary team of 50+ professionals  
- Implemented robust financial planning and cost-control measures  
- Built risk registers and mitigation strategies from tender through completion  
- Streamlined procurement, contract management and quality assurance  
- Introduced LEAN construction techniques on site  

**Outcome:** On-time delivery, significant cost savings, and a more efficient construction and commissioning process.
